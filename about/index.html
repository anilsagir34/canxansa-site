WHO WE

02 / Services

WHAT WE
DO.

Three transport modes, one accountable point of contact. Every shipment is planned end-to-end — mode selection, carrier booking, customs filing, in-transit follow-up and delivery confirmation.

AIR FREIGHT
General cargo, express and time-critical shipments. Direct and consolidated bookings out of major European, Turkish and Asian gateways.
SEA FREIGHT
FCL and LCL on the world's principal trade lanes — Europe, Mediterranean, Middle East and Far East. Port-to-port and door-to-door.
ROAD FREIGHT
Full and part loads across Europe, between Turkey and Europe and on regional Middle East corridors. Standard TIR and ATR documentation.

For corridors where one mode alone is sub-optimal — for example China to Europe via Turkey, or Central Asia to the EU — we structure multimodal solutions combining road, rail and sea legs under a single forwarding contract.

04 / Network

CARRIER
NETWORK.

CANXANSA does not operate its own fleet. Every shipment is executed by a carrier selected on three criteria: operational track record on the specific lane, commercial terms and regulatory compliance. Our network is grouped into four functional categories:

AIR CONSOLIDATORS
Global IATA-accredited consolidators handling general cargo, express and dangerous-goods air freight out of European and Turkish gateways.
EUROPEAN ROAD HAULIERS
Full-load and groupage operators with regular weekly departures between Turkey, the Balkans, Central Europe and the Benelux/Iberian range.
OCEAN PARTNERS
NVOCC and direct shipping-line relationships covering the principal trade lanes through Mediterranean, Northern European, Gulf and Far East ports.
REGIONAL SPECIALISTS
Lane-specific operators for the Middle East, Central Asia and Iraq corridors — areas where deep local knowledge matters more than global brand.

Carriers are reviewed periodically against on-time performance, claim ratio and documentation accuracy. Partners that drift on any of the three are replaced — that's the entire purpose of working with a forwarder rather than a single carrier.

06 / Compliance

LEGAL &
COMPLIANCE.

CANXANSA OÜ operates under Estonian and European Union law. Our forwarding activity is governed by our published General Transport Conditions; personal data is handled under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the Estonian Personal Data Protection Act.

We screen all parties against current EU and UN sanctions lists before accepting a booking, and we decline cargo that does not meet origin, dangerous-goods or destination-country regulations — in writing, before any commitment is made.

You can read our General Transport Conditions and our Privacy Policy for the full legal framework. For any compliance question, write to info@canxansa.com.
