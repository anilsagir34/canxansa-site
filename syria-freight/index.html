Common Questions

SYRIA
FREIGHT FAQ.

Which border crossing is used for Turkey–Syria road freight?

The primary commercial crossing is Cilvegözü (on the Turkish side) / Bab al-Hawa (on the Syrian side), located in Hatay province. This is the main gateway for goods entering northwest Syria including Aleppo, Idlib and Latakia. Other crossings exist for specific regions of Syria.

What goods are commonly shipped from Turkey to Syria?

Turkey is a major supplier of construction materials, food products, textiles, machinery, furniture, steel and consumer goods to Syria. Syria's post-conflict reconstruction process has driven significant growth in import demand, with Turkish exporters well-positioned to supply across all major sectors.

What documents are required to ship from Turkey to Syria?

Standard documents include commercial invoice, packing list, certificate of origin, CMR (road transport document) and customs declarations. Requirements may vary depending on commodity type and destination region. We advise on full documentation and any applicable permits before shipment.

Is air freight to Syria currently available?

Air freight to Damascus and Aleppo is available via selected carriers. Route availability and frequency continues to evolve as the situation normalises. We verify current flight options at time of booking and recommend the most reliable routing for your cargo.

Türkiye'den Suriye'ye nakliyat yapılabiliyor mu?

Evet. Cilvegözü/Bab al-Hawa sınır kapısı üzerinden Halep, Şam ve Lazkiye'ye kara nakliyesi aktif olarak gerçekleştirilmektedir. Suriye'deki yeniden yapılanma süreciyle birlikte ithalat talebi artmakta olup Türkiye bu süreçte önemli bir tedarik merkezi konumundadır. Teklif için formu doldurun, 1 saat içinde dönelim.
