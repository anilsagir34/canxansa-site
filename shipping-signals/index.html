What's Moving the Market
Fuel & Bunker
Bunker Prices Remain Elevated
VLSFO prices at Mediterranean ports are holding near $620/mt, keeping freight surcharges above seasonal norms. Carriers continue to pass through fuel cost increases on East Med routes.
Turkey — Europe
TIR Capacity Tightening
Road freight from Turkey to Germany and Benelux is seeing tighter capacity as summer produce shipments begin ramping up. Expect a 5-8% premium on spot rates through June.
China — Turkey
Ocean Rates Climbing
China to East Med ocean rates rose again this week. Ningbo-Mersin spot rates are up 5% WoW, driven by pre-summer restocking demand and blank sailings on the Asia-Med loop.
Middle East
Gulf Demand Steady
Turkey-Gulf lanes remain stable. Mersin-Jeddah and Mersin-Jebel Ali rates are flat, with consistent volume from Turkish FMCG and construction material exporters.
