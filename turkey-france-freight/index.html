Common Questions

TURKEY → FRANCE
FREIGHT FAQ.

How long does sea freight from Turkey to France take?

Sea freight from Istanbul or Mersin to Le Havre or Marseille typically takes 12–16 days. Marseille is closer geographically and often faster for southern France destinations.

What are the main ports for shipping to France from Turkey?

Le Havre is France's largest container port, handling the majority of Atlantic and North Sea traffic. Marseille serves the Mediterranean and southern France. Both offer excellent inland connections.

Is road freight from Turkey to France available?

Yes. Trucks travel via Bulgaria, Serbia and either Italy/Mont Blanc or Austria/Switzerland. Transit time is 5–7 days. Both FTL and LTL groupage services are available.

What documents are needed to ship from Turkey to France?

Standard documents include commercial invoice, packing list, bill of lading or airway bill, and a EUR.1 certificate if applicable. French customs (Douane) clearance is required for all imports.

Do you handle customs clearance in France?

Yes. We work with licensed customs brokers in France to handle import clearance, TVA (VAT), and applicable duties. We ensure all documentation meets French customs requirements.
